Tisha's Short Profile

My name is Tisha-an Lyka J. Ambal my friends call me Tisha but people also know me by my airname, Erin. I was born on May 15, 1994 in the beautiful and peaceful sunny island of Dumaguete. I am currently an Agribusiness Management student in UPLB and I aspire to be a Social Entrepreneur in God's time.

I studied highschool in SPCP and went on to college at The University of the Philippines Los Banos. Since I am a wide reader, my list of favorite books range from inspirational and motivational books like The Secret by Rhonda Byrne and The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho to Chic Lits like Liar Liar Pants on Fire by Meg Cabot and Twenties Girl by Sophie Kinsella. The most memorable quotation I learned from highschool is more of a mantra, and it goes like, This too shall pass. I try to recall this saying whenever I am feeling stressed and overwhelemd with school work. Here in college, the most memorable quoation that stuck to me so far is, No guts, no glory.
